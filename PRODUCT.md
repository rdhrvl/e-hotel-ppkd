## Users
* **Front Office Staff**: Working under time constraints at the front desk, requiring extremely fast check-in/out workflows and reservation handling with zero friction.
* **Admin / Manager**: Monitoring metrics (revenue, occupancy) and adjusting rooms, branches, and system settings.
* **Housekeeping**: Reviewing and updating task schedules dynamically from mobile/tablet devices while on the floor.
* **Finance**: Reconciling invoices, upfront payments, deposits, and settlement details.

## Product Purpose
An end-to-end, high-performance Hotel Management System (HMS) built using Laravel Livewire + TailwindCSS. The system manages room inventory, guest reservations, check-in/out states, billing, and housekeeping schedules. Success means high operational speed, low user input error, and smooth, transparent transaction handling (e.g., upfront payment integration).

## Brand Personality
* **Premium**: Clean, professional, and sophisticated look appropriate for high-end hospitality management.
* **Efficient**: Zero unnecessary visual clutter; every screen optimizes cognitive load and guides the user to action.
* **Trustworthy**: Secure, clear status badges, audit logs, and transparent payment calculations.

## Anti-references
* **Generic SaaS Clichés**: Overly rounded cards, soft wide shadows on inputs, tiny tracked all-caps eyebrows on every card/section.
* **Low-Contrast Grays**: Muted gray body text on off-white backgrounds that reduce readability for desk operators.
* **Complex Nested Modals**: Forcing users to click through layers of modal popups to complete booking or check-in.

## Design Principles
1. **Operational Efficiency First**: Minimize clicks and mouse movement; all primary workflows should be complete within 2–3 actions.
2. **Clear & High-Contrast Visual States**: Room and booking states must have high-contrast, unambiguous colors to prevent human errors at a glance.
3. **Data Transparency**: Show calculations (e.g. room totals, deposits, and settlements) explicitly as individual line items to reduce guest disputes.
4. **Resilient Forms & Feedback**: Prevent destructive or invalid actions through real-time feedback and state preservation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Target WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
* Contrast ratios for body text and placeholders must hit >= 4.5:1.
* Touch targets and interactive elements must have a minimum height/width of 40px.
